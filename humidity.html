Latitude vs. Humidity

Scatter Plot - Northern and Southern Hemispheres

According to the plot, humidity varies greatly at most latitudes. It appears that locations near the equator (between -10° S and 10° N) have higher levels of humidity. However, there is not enough evidence to determine whether there is a correlation between latitude and humidity.



Linear Regression - Northern Hemisphere

Based on the linear regression analysis, there is no correlation between latitude and humidity in the northern hemisphere. The r-squared value is 0.005.



Linear Regression - Southern Hemisphere

According to the linear regression plot, there is no correlation between latitude and humidity in the southern hemisphere. The r-squared value is 0.03.
