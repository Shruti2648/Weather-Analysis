Weather Analysis Project

Summary


The purpose of this project was to analyze the effect of geographical latitude on weather. Our dataset consisted of geographical location and weather data for over 500 cities worldwide, which we obtained from the OpenWeatherMap API. After assembling the dataset, we used Pandas and Matplotlib to plot city latitude versus various weather components. The components we focused on were temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.

We also conducted a correlation analysis to determine the direction and strength of the correlations we found. This site provides the source data and visualizations created for this project, as well as descriptions of correlations that we discovered.
